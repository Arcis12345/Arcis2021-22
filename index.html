What we do?

Team Arcis is the Official Aero Design of Dayananda Sagar College of Engineering, Bengaluru. We participate in the SAE Aero Design Challenge and AIAA DBF, two of the biggest student design competitions, held annually in USA. We represent India on the global stage, having brought innumerable accolades home and also hold the title of India's Leading Aero Design Team since 2015. Team Arcis functions as a start-up, raising funds via sponsorships and collaborations under CSR and as per the rules of the competition, we work independent of any professional or academic help.

Vision & Mission

To excel as a team of innovative engineers, leveraging UAV, UAS and other modern technologies for solving real world problems.

To inculcate discipline as well as professional skills in students so that they become responsible citizens who are able to flourish in their respective careers.
